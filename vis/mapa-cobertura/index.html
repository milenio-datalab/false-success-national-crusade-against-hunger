Coverage of the National Crusade Against Hunger

According to the official database of beneficiaries of the National Crusade Against Hunger, the states that have the largest concentration of people in extreme poverty and are malnourished have municipalities with coverages lower than 40%, while other states have municipalities with coverages over 100%. In the municipality of Juchipila (Zacatecas) the coverage of the target population is 1,516%.
